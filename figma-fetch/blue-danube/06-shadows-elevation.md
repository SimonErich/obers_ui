# TapTap Design System - Shadows & Elevation
## Source: Figma cover page node 1162:22675

## Elevation Scale

### Elevation 1 (Subtle)
- Shadow 1: X:0, Y:1, Blur:1, Spread:0, Color:#000000 @ 2% (0x05)
- Shadow 2: X:0, Y:2, Blur:4, Spread:0, Color:#000000 @ 4% (0x0A)

### Elevation 2 (Small)
- Shadow 1: X:0, Y:1, Blur:4, Spread:0, Color:#000000 @ 4% (0x0A)
- Shadow 2: X:0, Y:4, Blur:10, Spread:0, Color:#000000 @ 8% (0x14)

### Elevation 3 (Medium) — Used by toasts/messages
- Shadow 1: X:0, Y:2, Blur:20, Spread:0, Color:#000000 @ 4% (0x0A)
- Shadow 2: X:0, Y:8, Blur:32, Spread:0, Color:#000000 @ 8% (0x14)

### Elevation 4 (Large) — Used by modals/overlays
- Shadow 1: X:0, Y:8, Blur:20, Spread:0, Color:#000000 @ 6% (0x0F)
- Shadow 2: X:0, Y:24, Blur:60, Spread:0, Color:#000000 @ 12% (0x1F)

### Special: Brand Shadow (Cover decorative)
- 0px 10px 60px rgba(40, 145, 150, 0.2) — teal-tinted

## Surface Shapes
- All elevation surfaces use white background
- Surface border radius: 100px (circle/pill for elevation demo)
- Note: actual components use 4px radius
