## 1. Color System Overview

Arco Design includes 14 preset color families. Each family has 10 computed shades
generated by a dynamic HSV gradient algorithm. Shade 6 is the base color; shades 1-5
are progressively lighter tints; shades 7-10 are progressively darker.

### 1.1 Color Generation Algorithm

The algorithm operates in HSV color space with these parameters:
- **Hue step**: 2 degrees per shade
- **Hue direction**: For base hues 60-240, lighter shades shift hue down; darker shades shift up. Outside that range, the direction is reversed.
- **Saturation (light)**: Interpolates from base saturation down to 9 (min) over 5 steps
- **Saturation (dark)**: Interpolates from base saturation up to 100 (max) over 4 steps
- **Value/Brightness (light)**: Interpolates from base value up to 100 (max) over 5 steps
- **Value/Brightness (dark)**: Interpolates from base value down to 30 (min) over 4 steps

Source: `@arco-design/color` package (`palette.js`)
